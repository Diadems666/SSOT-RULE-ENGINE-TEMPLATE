About End of Trade Application

Streamlining Daily Reconciliation for Australian Bottle Shops

The End of Trade application is designed specifically for bottle shop managers to streamline the daily end-of-trade reconciliation process. This application helps manage daily takings, handle Australian currency denominations, balance safe and till floats, and track various payment methods.

Key Features
  • Calendar Interface: Navigate easily through daily financial records
  • Australian Currency Support: Complete support for all coin and banknote denominations
  • Safe Float Management: Track and balance safe float with target value of $1,500
  • Till Float Management: Manage till float with intelligent denomination calculation to maintain $500 value
  • Payment Method Tracking: Record and reconcile all payment methods including EFTPOS, cards, cash, and accounts
  • Variance Calculation: Automatically identify discrepancies between till readings and recorded payment methods
  • Daily Settlement: Mark each day's takings as settled once reconciliation is complete
How to Use

Navigate to the current day or select a date from the calendar. Fill in the denomination counts for your safe and till floats, record all payment method totals, and let the application handle the calculations and reconciliation. Once balanced, you can settle the day's takings to lock the record.
